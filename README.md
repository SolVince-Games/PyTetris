## Controls
**left rotate**: q or RSHIFT
**right rotate**: e or end
**move left**: a or left
**move right**: d or right
**soft down**: s or down
**hard down**: space
**hold**: h
**pause**: enter
**quit**: esc
**toggle ghost**: g
**toggle colour**: c
**scale 1 (256x224)**: 1
**scale 2 (512x448)**: 2
**scale 3 (768x672)**: 3
**scale 4 (1024x896)**: 4
